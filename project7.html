Final project

    Goal

    After extensive brainstorming my group decided that the final project would be to design and create a subwoofer inspired by Bowers and Wilkins' Nautilus design as introduced in project 1. It came as no surprise that many enthusiasts have done comparable projects before, for example Nickolae's 3D printed, upside-down version from which we will be drawing heavy inspiration from.

    Our version, however, will utilize molding and casting techniques to create the spiral shell housing. The bracket to hold the subwoofer will be cut from wood with a CNC machine similarly to Nickolae's project but will incorporate a pressfit design in some manner. Finally, the feet suports will be 3D printed and the whole assembly screwed into place with from-the-shelf screws.

    Concept

    To begin with, I made a couple of simple sketches that, although far from accurate and might not represent the final product, will lay the necessary conceptual groundwork for the CAD design.
